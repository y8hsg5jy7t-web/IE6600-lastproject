Los Angeles ZIP Code Desirability Dashboard

Visualization of affordability, income, education, and livability

Methodology

Desirability Score =

(Bachelor’s Degree × 0.20)
+ Income × 0.25
+ Home Value × 0.20
+ Rent × 0.10
+ Parks × 0.10
+ Poverty × 0.15
  • Higher income, education, home value, and park access increase desirability.
  • Higher rent decreases desirability due to affordability pressure.
  • Higher poverty decreases desirability due to economic instability.
  • Home value balances housing quality against cost burden.

Null Handling: Missing values were treated as zero to prevent artificial inflation due to incomplete data.
